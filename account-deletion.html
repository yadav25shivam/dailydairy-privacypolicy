Dairy Khata – Account & Data Deletion Request

Dairy Khata allows you to request permanent deletion of your account and associated data. Please read the steps below.

How to request deletion

Send an email to support@dairy-khata.com with the subject line: "Account Deletion Request – Dairy Khata".

Your email must include:

  • Your registered phone number (the number you used to sign in)
  • Your name / dairy name

After we verify the request belongs to you, we will delete your account.

What will be deleted

  • Your profile information (name, phone number)
  • Your dairy records / milk collection and payment ledger data you created in the app
  • Business details you added in Dairy Khata

What may be kept (and why)

We may keep records that are legally required for accounting / tax / audit purposes, such as transaction history that involves other parties. If we keep this data, we remove your personal identifiers and keep it only in an anonymized / aggregated form.

How long it takes

We will complete account deletion (and personal data deletion) within 30 days after we confirm your request.

Uninstall vs. deletion

You can uninstall the Dairy Khata app at any time. However, uninstalling the app from your phone does not automatically delete the data stored in our systems. To permanently delete your stored data, you must follow the request process above.
